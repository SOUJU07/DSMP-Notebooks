Practice
1. find the average battery capacity and the average primary rear camera resolution for all smartphones with a price greater than or equal to 100000.

SELECT ROUND(AVG(battery_capacity),2) AS avg_battery_capacity,ROUND(AVG(primary_camera_rear),2) AS avg_primary_camera_rear
FROM smartphones
WHERE price>=100000


2. Find the average internal memory capacity of smartphone that have a refresh rate of 120Hz or higher and a front facing camera resolution greater than or equal to 20 megapixels.

SELECT ROUND(AVG(internal_memory),2) as avg_internal_memory
FROM smartphones
WHERE primary_camera_front >=20 and refresh_rate>=120

3.Find the number of smartphones with 5g capabilities.

SELECT COUNT(*)
FROM smartphones
WHERE has_5g = 'TRUE'
